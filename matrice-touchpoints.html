Concept mockup · Product × Timing Matrix

Pushing the right product at the right point in the race journey

This is a working model for the Product × Timing Matrix already on our Q3 2026 roadmap (foundation item, low effort / high impact). Rows are the touchpoints we've already mapped — confirmation email, newsletters, myDS, start list, results page, post-race sequence. Columns are the timing windows we already use internally (4MPR, 2MPR, Race day, and the post-race sequence through J+21). Each cell decides whether to push Fit for Life Coach, the Fit for Life Magazine, or stay brand-only. The goal over the next few months: run this matrix by hand, then hand it to a signal engine that decides per athlete.

01 — The principle

Two axes, one content decision per cell

Rows: our already-identified touchpoints, from the confirmation email to the post-race sequence. Columns: distance to race day — including the post-race window, which is where most of our conversion upside is still untapped. Each populated cell names the product to push and a one-line example of that moment's teaser.

04 — Vision

Next: the matrix run by a signal engine

Longer term, the cell isn't fixed in advance — it's computed per athlete. The engine builds directly on our planned athlete data collection and persona work, scoring product fit and timing for that specific person.

Input

Athlete profile

  • Race goal & experience level (set at registration)
  • Acquisition source & channel
  • Gap between goal and result (auto-calculated post-event)
  • Multi-event history via Strava / Garmin
  • Email, newsletter & myDS engagement
→
Processing

Signal engine

Builds on the planned persona segmentation and conversion-based matrix to score Coach vs. Magazine affinity, and calibrates timing and tone for that athlete.

→
Output

Personalized touchpoint

The right product, the right message, on the right channel — generated automatically from the matrix.

↺ Feedback loop: every interaction — opens, clicks, gap-to-goal, Strava sync — feeds back into the athlete profile and sharpens the score for the next race.
05 — Roadmap

From manual matrix to signal engine

In progress · Q3 2026 Phase 1

Ship the matrix

Touchpoint mapping and channel strategy are already underway; this deck turns the planned matrix into a working model to pilot.

Planned · Q4 2026 Phase 2

Wire up tracking & data

Track conversions per touchpoint (OnReg, articles, partners) and enrich profiles: race goal, experience level, multi-event history.

Planned · Q4 2026 → Q1 2027 Phase 3

Build the persona layer

Turn athlete segmentation and the conversion-based matrix into a defined upselling strategy per persona.

Proposed next step Phase 4

Automate with an AI signal engine

Score athletes live and let the engine choose the product and message per cell — not yet on the roadmap; what this deck is asking to scope.
